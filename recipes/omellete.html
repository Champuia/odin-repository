Omellete

Description

This recipe is by the great Leonardo DiCaprio. He was a very talented cook when his mother was alive.

This recipe will make your heart beat faster as it is the most exquisite and decorated omellete you will ever see.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • cooking oil
  • frying pan
  • salt

Steps

  1. Heat the frying pan to 500 Degrees
  2. Crack the eggs in to a bowl
  3. add salt to the egg and stir it for a good mixture and texture
  4. oil the pan
  5. After the pan is hot enough pour in the eggs
  6. Cook it till the bottom is brown and flip it
  7. cook the other side till brown too
  8. enjoy your most exquisite egg of all time
